Cohort

2020— 21

Cohort is a group research and experiment project from 15 photographers, undertaken from 2019—21.

b.1993
Cigarettes for poems is a book that I was going to publish of poems, written whilst drunk on my iPhone, but because I'm not good at iCloud I lost them. Now cigarettes for poems is work made reflecting on that feeling of losing the poems, and how the feeling of recklessness and reflection on adolescence feels now.
